## 📋 PAGE STRUCTURE & SECTIONS

### SECTION 1: HERO
**Headline:** "1,000 on-brand ads. Minutes, not months."

**Subheadline:** Instant Studio deploys managed AI workflows - Creative Sequences - that automate ad production, animation, localisation, and variant generation across your APAC markets. 10-50x the output at a third of the cost, with your brand guardrails built in.

**Primary CTA:** Talk to Our Team
**Secondary CTA:** See Our Work

**Design Notes:** Full-bleed background, bold kinetic energy, motion/animation that reinforces brand feel without blocking page load

---

### SECTION 2: PROBLEM
**Headline:** "The content treadmill is relentless."

**Copy:** 
Content demand will grow 5x by 2027. Headcount will not.

Your team is producing assets one at a time while clients expect AI-powered creative yesterday. Your "personalised" content is just name insertion that nobody shares. Your global campaigns require manual adaptation for every market—new models, new locations, new CTA copy—and the production timeline keeps slipping.

Meanwhile, 35% of agencies cite compromised creative quality as their top AI challenge. The tools that promise speed deliver output that screams "AI made this." Consumer backlash against generic AI content is accelerating.

You need scale without sacrificing the work.

**Design Notes:** Dark Espresso background, large stat callouts (5x, 35%), typographic moments, weighty and urgent tone

---

### SECTION 3: AGITATE
**Headline:** "Your competitors are not waiting."

**Copy:**
69% of Fortune 500 companies are already using AI-generated video for marketing. 91% of agencies are using or exploring generative AI. The window is closing on early-mover advantage.

You've probably seen the data about how 95% of generative AI pilots fail (MIT, 2025). But here's what the 5% who succeed do differently: they buy rather than build: working with specialised vendors succeeds about 67% of the time, while internal builds succeed only one-third as often.

The question is not whether to use AI for creative production. It is whether you will execute with a partner who has already solved the hard problems—or learn them the expensive way.

**Design Notes:** Continue dark/urgent tone, stat callouts, pull-quotes as visual anchors

**Copy:**

We operate as your production studio—handling creative direction, technical orchestration, compliance, and delivery. You direct the vision. We handle AI.

**Design Notes:** Light Paper background (relief moment), workflow diagram showing Global Campaign → Local Adaptation → Multi-market Delivery, Orange accents return, CTA: "Book a Custom Demo for Your APAC Rollout"

### SECTION 5: VALUE PROPOSITIONS
**Headline:** "What makes us different"

**Four Value Props:**

**1. Bespoke creative, not template-driven.**
Instant Studio creates video content with custom creative direction, real talent and production values that don't look AI-generated. Not stock avatars. Not generic output that screams automation.

**2. Data-aware, not just personalised.**
Instant Studio video is informed by business data: buying habits, ad interactions, CRM records and lifecycle signals. Content that knows who it's talking to.

**3. Locally relevant, not just localised.**
Instant Studio converts global campaigns into locally relevant creative: adapting talent, locations, cultural context, CTA copy and animations. Not just swapping text and resizing assets.

**4. Production partner, not a login.**
Instant Studio operates as your AI production studio: handling creative direction, technical orchestration, compliance, and delivery. Not a self-serve platform, but a creative partner working alongside you.

**Design Notes:** Card or grid layout, each prop needs icon or visual shorthand, keep scannable—avoid wall-of-text

### SECTION 8: FAQ / OBJECTION HANDLING
**Headline:** "Questions we hear."

**Q1: Our clients aren't ready for AI.**
A: Many clients now expect AI acceleration. Our client's marketing lead said "Just do it." The question is not readiness—it is whether you will be the agency that leads or the one that is asked why you didn't.

**Q2: The output won't be good enough quality.**
A: We are not an AI tool generating generic content. You own the creative vision; we execute with the precision your brand demands.

**Q3: We don't have time to learn a new tool.**
A: You don't learn anything. Instant Studio operates the entire pipeline. Your team provides the data and approves the creative. We handle everything else.

**Q4: What about compliance and data privacy?**
A: Our workflows are pre-vetted by enterprise legal and compliance teams across multiple global markets. Every piece of talent and celebrity asset features fully documented consent. Your data stays secure and in-region on AWS—and we maintain a strict commitment that client data is never used to train our models.

**Q5: This seems expensive.**
A: Compared to traditional production: 5,000 unique personalised videos would cost millions—or be impossible. Start with a pilot to prove the model.

**Design Notes:** Accordion or expandable format. Clean, scannable. This section does heavy lifting for Enterprise Buyers in consideration phase.

## 🎯 USER JOURNEY & CONVERSION PATHS

### RUSH BUYER JOURNEY (1-2 week cycle)
1. Lands on hero section
2. Reads headline + validates problem in Problem section
3. Sees urgency in Agitate section (competitors moving, 95% fail rate)
4. Clicks "Talk to Our Team" CTA to schedule capability call
5. Conversion target: 12-15% of traffic

### ENTERPRISE BUYER JOURNEY (3-6 month cycle)
1. Lands on hero section
2. Validates problem + urgency
3. Deep-dives into Solution section to understand service model
4. Explores Value Propositions and Case Studies for proof
5. Reads FAQ to address compliance, quality, cost concerns
6. Clicks "See Our Work" for case study exploration OR "Talk to Our Team" when ready for demo
7. Conversion target: 6-10% of traffic

## 📱 KEY UX REQUIREMENTS

**Performance:**
- Page load time: <3 sec (4G)
- Mobile usability: >90 (Google Core Web Vitals)
- Interaction to Paint: <100ms
- Cumulative Layout Shift: <0.1

**Mobile Considerations:**
- Hero headline must be readable and impactful at mobile scale (320px)
- Stats and proof points should stack cleanly
- FAQ accordion is native-friendly
- CTAs must be thumb-accessible (min 44px)

**Navigation:**
- Sticky header with logo + primary CTA ("Talk to Our Team") always accessible
- Consider minimal nav that scrolls to key sections vs no nav (test both)

**Accessibility:**
- WCAG 2.1 Level AA minimum
- Color contrast: 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- Semantic HTML, alt text for images
- Keyboard navigation throughout

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM & STYLING

### COLOR PALETTE (Per Client Brief)
- **Outrageous Orange:** Primary/hero color (bold, energetic)
- **Espresso:** Deep contrast (dark brown/maroon, grounds orange, section backgrounds)
- **Lilac:** Accent/highlight (unexpected, modern, buttons)
- **Paper:** Background/light (warm off-white, primary page background)
- **Ink:** Text/dark UI (near-black charcoal, primary body text)

### TYPOGRAPHY (Per Client Brief)
- **Headlines:** Hubot Sans SemiCondensed (bold, condensed, high-impact, set large)
- **Body Copy:** DM Sans (clean geometric sans-serif, excellent readability, warm)
- **Eyebrow/Labels:** DM Mono (monospaced, technical feel, section labels, category tags)

### DESIGN PRINCIPLES
- **Tone:** Bold, kinetic, resonant
- **Layout:** Large typographic moments, generous whitespace
- **Section Contrast:** Dark Espresso alternating with light Paper sections for visual breaks
- **Philosophy:** "The page itself should feel like proof that Instant Studio produces high-quality creative, not generic AI output"
