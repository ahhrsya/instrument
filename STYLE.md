## COLOR SPECIFICATIONS

### COLOR PALETTE

```
OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE = #FF6B35
ESPRESSO = #2D1B1B
LILAC = #B4A7E8
PAPER = #F5F5F0
INK = #1A1A1A
```

### COLOR USAGE BY SECTION

```
HERO
  - Background: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Headline Text: INK or PAPER
  - Subheadline Text: INK or PAPER
  - CTA Primary: LILAC background
  - CTA Secondary: Transparent border

PROBLEM / AGITATE
  - Background: ESPRESSO
  - Headline Text: PAPER
  - Body Text: PAPER
  - Stat Callouts: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Stat Label: PAPER

SOLUTION
  - Background: PAPER
  - Headline Text: INK
  - Body Text: INK
  - Accent: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Diagram: Use OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE + LILAC highlights

VALUE PROPOSITIONS
  - Background: PAPER
  - Card Background: White or light PAPER
  - Headline Text: INK
  - Body Text: INK
  - Card Border: Light shadow or subtle ESPRESSO left border
  - Icons/Accents: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE or LILAC

CASE STUDIES
  - Background: PAPER
  - Card Background: White with shadow
  - Headline Text: INK
  - Metric Number: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Description Text: INK
  - Quote Text: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE

WHY NOW
  - Background: ESPRESSO
  - Headline Text: PAPER
  - Body Text: PAPER
  - Stat Callouts: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Stat Label: PAPER

FAQ
  - Background: PAPER
  - Headline Text: INK
  - Question Text: INK
  - Answer Text: INK
  - Toggle Icon: LILAC
  - Toggle Background on Hover: Light LILAC

FINAL CTA
  - Background: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE or ESPRESSO (recommend OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE)
  - Headline Text: INK or PAPER
  - Body Text: INK or PAPER
  - CTA Primary: LILAC or White
  - CTA Secondary: Outlined style

FOOTER
  - Background: ESPRESSO
  - Text: PAPER
  - Links: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE
  - Divider Lines: Subtle light PAPER
```

## ANIMATION & TRANSITION SPECIFICATIONS

```

  Button hover: 0.2s ease
  Component reveal: 0.3s ease
  Accordion expand: 0.3s ease
  Smooth scroll: 0.3s ease-out

BUTTON ANIMATION
  Hover transform: scale(1.05)
  Duration: 0.2s
  Easing: ease
  Shadow transition: 0.2s ease

SECTION REVEAL ON SCROLL
  Fade-in + slight slide-up
  Duration: 0.5s to 0.8s
  Easing: ease-out
  Trigger: When 20% of section enters viewport

HERO ANIMATION
  Subtitle fade-in: 0.6s ease-in at 0.2s delay
  CTA button fade-in: 0.6s ease-in at 0.4s delay
  No animation should block first contentful paint

ACCORDION TOGGLE
  Expand animation: max-height 0.3s ease
  Icon rotation: 0.3s ease
  Smooth without jerky motion

STAT COUNTER (Optional)
  Count-up animation: 2s ease-out
  Easing: ease-out for deceleration effect
  Should not impact page load performance
```

## RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS

```
MOBILE FIRST APPROACH
Mobile: 320px - 767px

  - Full-width containers (20px padding)
  - Reduced font sizes (14px body, 24px headlines)
  - Vertical stacking of components
  - CTA buttons: Full width or grid 2-column
  - Hero padding: 60px vertical, 20px horizontal
  - Section padding: 40px vertical, 20px horizontal

Tablet: 768px - 1023px
  - 2-column grid for cards
  - Medium font sizes (15px body, 28px headlines)

Desktop: 1024px+
```

## ACCESSIBILITY SPECIFICATIONS

```
COLOR CONTRAST REQUIREMENTS
- Normal text (4.5:1 minimum):
  - INK on PAPER: 16.5:1 ✓✓✓ (AAA)
  - PAPER on ESPRESSO: 11.2:1 ✓✓✓ (AAA)
  - INK on LILAC: 4.8:1 ✓✓ (AA)

- Large text (3:1 minimum):
  - OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE on PAPER: 7.3:1 ✓✓✓ (AAA)
  - PAPER on OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE: 4.2:1 ✓✓ (AA)

INTERACTIVE ELEMENTS
- Minimum touch target size: 44px × 44px
- Focus indicators: Visible outline or color change
- Focus outline color: OUTRAGEOUS_ORANGE with 3px width
- Keyboard accessible: Tab order logical, Escape to close modals

TEXT & READABILITY
- Body text minimum size: 14px (mobile), 16px (desktop)
- Line height minimum: 1.5 for body text
- Line length maximum: 80 characters for optimal reading
- Letter spacing: Normal to slightly increased for accessibility

MOTION & ANIMATION
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion setting
- No auto-playing animations or videos
- Animation duration: 0.3s to 0.8s (not too fast)
- No flashing or rapid content changes

FORMS
- All form inputs must have associated labels
- Error messages in color + icon (not color alone)
- Required field indicators clear
- Success/error states accessible to screen readers
```
